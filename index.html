Jojo's Bizarre Adventure

A Multi-Generational Epic by Hirohiko Araki.

Brought to the world by the mangaka Hirohiko Araki, Jojo's Bizarre Adventere is not only his most famous work, but one of the most influential manga to date. Follow the adventures of the Joestar family, a family of martial artists and psychics, as they fight against the supernatural and save their homes and the world. Be thrilled as they fight against Vampires, Serial Killers, and even ancient gods. The series has eight parts to date, each more bizarre than the last!


Phantom Blood

Part 1

The life of Jonathan Joestar takes a strange turn when the dastardly Dio Brando enters and a mask of stone enter his life. Follow Jonathan as he fights vampires, zombies, and even his adopted brother, Dio.

Battle Tendency

Part 2

Joseph Joestar, the grandson of Jonathan Joestar, finds himself in trouble when ancient vampire gods known as the Piller Men reawaken. He'll need his smarts, his martial arts, and many allies if he wants to defeat them and save the world.

Stardust Crusaders

Part 3

Strong Psychic powers awaken in Jotaro Kujo, grandson of Joseph Joestar, and he's going to need them alongside allies with these same strange powers, to defeat an evil from the past and save his mother.

Diamond is Unbreakable

Part 4

A dark secret lurks in the Japanese town of Morioh. Can Josuke Higashikata and his friends figure it out before the serial killer Yoshikage Kira finishes them off?

Vento Aureo

Part 5

Giorno Giovanna, a teenager with strange ties to the Joestar Bloodline, seeks to rise through the ranks of the notorious gang Passione, and become the leader.

Stone Ocean

Part 6

Jolyne Cujoh, daughter of Jotaro, is falsely accused of a crime and sentenced to prison. But things go from bad to worse as a plan set in motion by an enemy from her father's past begins to unfold that could mean the end of the entire world.

Steel Ball Run

Part 7

Johnny Joestar, a paraplegic horse rider, joins a race across the country to get closer to a man he believes can fix his legs, but uncovers a conspiracy that goes as deep as the President of the United States!

Jojolion

Part 8

A strange man crawls from the ground, stricken with amnesia, and must find the secrets to his past. Join him as he uncovers not only who he is, but a race of Rock Humans, who may have a sinister plot brewing.

  • About the Mangaka

    Born June 7, 1960 in Sendai, Miyagi, Japan, Hirohiko Araki is a mangaka with many works, such as Baoh and Gorgeous Irene. He most well known for his manga, Jojo's Bizarre Adventure, which has been running for almost 30 years. He has had work with Gucci, and some of his artwork even made it into the Louvre.
